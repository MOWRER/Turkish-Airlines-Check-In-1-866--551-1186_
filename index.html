The Ultimate Guide to Turkish Airlines Check-In

Planning to fly with Turkish Airlines? Whether you’re embarking on a short domestic hop or a long-haul international journey, understanding the airline’s check-in options and timelines is essential for a stress-free start. Turkish Airlines provides a range of convenient check-in methods—online, mobile app, self-service kiosks, and airport counters—alongside useful add-on services. Below is a comprehensive, keyword-rich breakdown to help you navigate the Turkish Airlines check-in process with ease.

1. Online Check-In

Window: Opens 24 hours before scheduled departure and closes 90 minutes prior for both domestic and international flights.

How to Access: Visit the Turkish Airlines official website and click “Check-in.” Enter your booking reference or e-ticket number, along with your last name.

Benefits:

  • Seat Selection: Choose or change your seat assignment in real time.
  • Extras & Upgrades: Add checked baggage, purchase extra legroom, or upgrade your class if eligible.
  • Digital Boarding Pass: Download and email yourself the PDF boarding pass or save it to your mobile wallet for hassle-free airport entry.

Pro Tip: Complete online check-in as soon as the window opens to secure preferred seats—especially important for families traveling together or for extra legroom.

2. Mobile App Check-In

Window: Identical to online check-in (24 hr–90 min prior).

App: The Turkish Airlines mobile app (iOS & Android) offers a streamlined check-in widget.

Features:

  • Push Notifications for flight status and gate changes.
  • Boarding Pass Wallet Integration for Apple Wallet and Google Pay.
  • Real-Time Baggage Tracking (where supported).
  • Flight Reminders and seat-map views.

Why Use It? Mobile check-in maximizes on-the-go convenience; you can finalize everything on your phone, skip the counters, and proceed directly to bag drop or security if you have no hold luggage.

3. Self-Service Kiosk Check-In

Availability: At select major airports (e.g., Istanbul, London Heathrow, Frankfurt, New York JFK).

Window: Kiosk check-in typically opens 12 hours before departure and closes 45 minutes prior for domestic flights, and 60 minutes prior for international flights.

Process:

  • Scan your passport or enter your booking reference.
  • Select or change seats on the touchscreen map.
  • Print your paper boarding pass and baggage tags.
  • Attach printed tags and proceed to Bag Drop.

Quick Tip: Kiosks are ideal for passengers traveling with checked baggage who still want a speedy, line-free experience—just remember to allow extra time to tag and drop your bags.

4. Airport Counter Check-In

Opening Times: Counters open 3 hours before departure for both domestic and international flights.

Closing Times:

  • Domestic flights: 45 minutes before departure.
  • International flights: 60 minutes before departure.

What’s Offered:

  • Assisted Check-In for families, groups, and passengers requiring special assistance.
  • Document Verification for visas and passports.
  • Unaccompanied Minor Services and Pet Check-In.

Recommendation: Arrive at least 2 hours before domestic flights and 3 hours before international flights to accommodate security screening, baggage drop, and any unexpected delays.

5. Special Services & Add-Ons

  • Priority Check-In Lanes: Available for Business Class, Miles&Smiles Elite/Elite Plus, and Star Alliance Gold members at select airports.
  • Family & Group Check-In: Larger groups (10+ passengers) and families traveling with infants can request a dedicated counter.
  • Accessibility Assistance: Wheelchair services and mobility aid handling must be requested at least 48 hours in advance during booking or via customer service at +1-866-551-1186 (USA).
  • Extra Baggage & Sports Equipment: Prepay for additional allowance via the website or at the counter to save time and money.

6. Baggage Drop & Deadlines

  • Carry-On Only: After online/mobile/kiosk check-in, proceed directly to security—no need to visit the counter.
  • Checked Baggage: Tag your bags at the kiosk or have your tags printed at the counter, then drop them at the Bag Drop desk.

Cut-Offs:

  • Domestic: Bag Drop closes 45 minutes before departure.
  • International: Bag Drop closes 60 minutes before departure.

Oversized Items: Sports gear, musical instruments, and pet carriers should arrive at least 3 hours before departure to allow for special handling.

7. Travel Document Requirements

  • Passport Control: Ensure your passport has at least 6 months’ validity beyond your return date.
  • Visa Verification: Check visa requirements for each destination; some countries require visas on arrival or pre-approval.
  • COVID-19 / Health Declarations: Follow any current health screening or digital pass protocols via the Turkish Airlines app.
